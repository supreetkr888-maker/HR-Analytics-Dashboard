
# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 🚀 Overview

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze employee data and uncover key insights related to attrition, salary trends, job roles, and workforce demographics.

The dashboard helps organizations make **data-driven HR decisions** to improve employee retention and optimize workforce planning.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Excel / CSV Dataset
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Visualization

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

* Total Employees: **1460**
* Attrition Count: **236**
* Attrition Rate: **16.2%**
* Average Age: **37**
* Average Salary: **6.5K**
* Average Years at Company: **7**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 🔹 Attrition Analysis

* Higher attrition observed in certain job roles like **Laboratory Technician** and **Sales Executive**
* Majority attrition occurs in the **26–35 age group**

### 🔹 Salary Insights

* Employees earning **below 5K** show the highest attrition
* Lower salary slabs are directly linked with higher employee turnover

### 🔹 Education & Job Role Trends

* Employees from **Life Sciences and Medical backgrounds** form the majority
* Certain job roles contribute disproportionately to attrition

### 🔹 Experience Analysis

* Employees with fewer years at the company are more likely to leave
* Attrition decreases as experience increases
